market segmentation is : deviding product marketing into for smaller more specific consumers who share similar charastrictrics making groups 


Process 

broad market -> segmentation -> target marketing


main types 

1- demographic --> deviding market based on demographic chrastrictics like age,income
2- geographic --> devinding market based on geographic like region,countries neighborhood
3- psychographic --> devinding market based on social life,lifestyle emotional level
4- behavioral --> analyzing how customer react to products  habits, brandloyalty

Implement

1- conduct market research 
2- identify and define market segment
3- evaluate the segments --> size of segment, growth potential
4- develop segment specific stratigies 
5- implement and monitor

Benifits 

1-target marketing focusing marketing to a specific groups which make it easier and more efficient
2- customer satisfaction 
3- product development for different purposes
4- cost efficiency
5- competitive advantage

Examples 

Spotify , cocacola

Limitaions 

1- complexity and cost 

2- over segmentation

3- potential for stereotyping

4- difficulty in measuring effectivness
